  <Tab title="Meeting Preparation">
    
    Meeting Preparation simplifies your pre-meeting tasks by automatically generating guided talking points and potential follow-up questions. Just input your agenda into watsonx and receive structured conversation prompts.
    > This currently supports only watsonx.ai related guided conversations.

    <Tip>
    This was developed as part of 2024 watsonx Challenge and **reused** here.
    </Tip>
    <img height="300" src="/images/meeting prep.png" />

    ## Try Meeting Preparation

    <Steps>
        <Step title="First Step">
            Provide an agenda to receive a structured conversation designed to help you prepare for your meeting. You can select quick-presets as well.

            Click on **I have a meeting with lenovo I need help in discussing pricing and sizing of watsonx.ai ->**
        </Step>
        <Step title="Second Step">
        This will give the possible questions and answers to that questions that you may get from the meeting.
        </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Meeting Insights">
    
    Upload your Teams meeting transcript to receive a ready-to-send email summary of key takeaways and action items. With just one click, you can generate professional meeting minutes, formatted for easy sharing. With the watsonx Orchestrate integration, you can directly send the emails to the recipients.
    
    <Check>
    Our Ecosystem Engineering Lab team extensively uses this feature to summarize our meetings, saving us 15 minutes each time.
    </Check>

    <img height="300" src="/images/meeting insights.png" />

    ## Try Meeting Insights with the sample data

    <Steps>
        <Step title="First Step">
            <Card title="Download Teams Transcript" icon="link" href="https://github.ibm.com/ecosystem-engineering-lab/opex-apis/raw/main/content/Team%20meeting.docx">
            Download the teams transcript from one of our team meeting.
            </Card>
        </Step>
        <Step title="Second Step">
            Click on **Upload**, and upload the transcript and click on **Get Insights**.
        </Step>
        <Step title="Third Step">
            You will see an email drafted with subject and body containing the summary of your teams meeting. You can edit anything if the LLM missed any insights. The editing can be done in markdown format and it will be automatically formatted in the final email that gets sent out.
        </Step>
        <Step title="Fourth Step">
            Enter the To, CC and BCC fields, you can enter the email ids in any of the two formats mentioned below:
            - Enter and email id and click **Enter**. This will clear the text box and add the name below. You can go on adding in this fashion.
            - Or alternatively, enter multiple email ids separated by **;**(semi-colan). Example: 
            `sumeet_kapoor@in.ibm.com;manoj.jahgirdar@in.ibm.com`
        </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email Summary">

    Simply paste your email content to receive a concise summary highlighting all the critical points. This feature allows you to quickly understand the essentials and respond efficiently.

    <Warning>Due to the limitation of obtaining an API key for Microsoft Outlook with our IBM ID, this copy-paste method is currently the best available option that we have implemented.</Warning>

    <img height="300" src="/images/email summarizer.png" />

    ## Try Email Summary

    <Steps>
        <Step title="First Step">
            Simply paste any email content in the text field.
        </Step>
        <Step title="Second Step">
            You can select the summary type as either **Short** or **Detailed**
        </Step>
        <Step title="Third Step">
            And click on **Summarize**
        </Step>
    </Steps>
    <Info>
    We actively incorporate feedbacks! One of the feedback that we got from our initial users was to include top 3 responses that can be sent as a response for the email, we'll be incorporating this soon!
    </Info>
