<Tabs>
  <Tab title="one-pager standardization">
    Generate polished one-pagers in 10–15 minutes. You can fill in the text
    boxes in one liner and Enhance with watsonx.ai would enrich the text using
    contextual knowledge and export them to PowerPoint with one click.

    <Info>
      A recent survey conducted on the #gsi-lab-all channel revealed that
      creating a one-pager typically takes 30 minutes to over an hour. With this
      tool, you can generate polished, presentation-ready one-pagers in just
      10–15 minutes—a time savings of 50–75%
    </Info>

    <img height="300" src="/images/1-pager.png" />
    
    ## Try one-pager standardization
    <Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        Select one-pager template, it can be **Data** or **Automation**.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Enter the title for your one pager.
        >Note: The one-pager will be saved by this name
    </Step>
    <Step title="Third Step">
        Enter a one liner or a brief description in **Business Challenges**, **Value proposition**, **technologies**, **stack**, **asset highlights**, **benefits**, **client success (optional)** 
        >These fields can be enhanced with watsonx.ai
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fourth Step">
        Finally enter the field **Why IBM** 
        >These field is not enhanced with watsonx.ai
    </Step>
    <Step title="Fifth Step">
        You can use the **Enhance with wx.ai** feature to proof read your fields and then you can go to the next page to fill the Target Audiance and IBM Contact, Link to assets section and finally download the PowerPoint presentation.
    </Step>
    </Steps>


  </Tab>
  <Tab title="2-minutes video">
    Create professional 2-minute videos in under 5 minutes. Choose a voice,
    enter a title, and if available, upload a 1-pager. The fields for Intro,
    Problem, Solution, Outcomes, and Call to Actions will be automatically
    populated based on the 1-pager. You can also upload images to include in the
    video. Finally, download the completed video.

    <Info>
      A 2-min video is an ask from every seller. This tool helps in reducing
      the time and efforts required to generate a 2-min video.
    </Info>
